About me

Hi, Im Jack, a medieval reeanctor from Bavaria Germany.

I have always had a passion for history and medieval times and last year I met a Sword Master from England and have been training under him ever since.

I particapate in show fights, full contact battles, point fights, and tournaments. I am primarly a sword and shield fighter and skirmisher in battles. My style is early medieval and I dress as a Norman Knight.

I am also part of reeanactment group called Legion of the Stag. We are based in Ulm and travel all over Germany for events. We also do events in Italy and England.

If you would like to book me or my group for an event or any other collaboration, head on over to my contact page to get in touch. I can also be reached through the social media links below.
